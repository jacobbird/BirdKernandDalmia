Anu Dalmia M.S.

       Anu Dalmia is a (co owner/partner at Bird Kern Dalmia and Associates), speech language pathologist with 15 years of experience in school- based, home based, and clinical based practice. 

       She received her B.S. And post professional MSC degree in communication disorders from AYJ national institute and Nair Hospital in India.

       Anu Dalmia is licensed in the state of California and in India to provide speech language assessments and treatment in school, home and clinical settings. She is also a member of the California Speech Language and Hearing Association (CSHA), and American Speech Language Hearing Association (ASHA). She has experience with pediatrics, adults and geriatric population. Her specialty is with Autism, Apraxia, Fluency, Feeding, Language delays, Articulation, and Social skills and group therapy but it's not limited to these disorders. She has been actively and successfully running social groups for different age groups. Anu is PROMPT trained and enjoys Early Intervention. She is also actively involved in giving workshops on many speech,language and communication related topics. She is often called to consult with teachers at different school districts and private schools and assists in giving creative ideas and suggestions for parents and staff.
